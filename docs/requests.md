# Requests

New content requests are handled through Plex's native [Watchlist](https://support.plex.tv/articles/universal-watchlist/) system. Simply add movies or tv shows to your Watchlist inside of Plex and they will be added to Plex shortly thereafter.

![Watchlist Button](../assets/images/requests_plex_watchlist_button.jpg)

## Requirements

You need to have your Watchlist set to at least "Friends Only" and not "Private".

![Watchlist Settings](../assets/images/requests_plex_watchlist.jpg)
